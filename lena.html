About Lena Africa

Lena is a pioneering edtech company founded by passionate Africans to transform learning across the continent. Our flagship solution, the Lena Learning and School Management System, provides a powerful suite of tools tailored for schools, educators, and students in Africa.

This comprehensive platform:

  • Simplifies teaching by enabling instructors to host courses and assign work
  • Facilitates seamless communication between teachers and students
  • Empowers learners to actively engage from anywhere
  • Helps schools efficiently manage classes, resources, and staff

Born during the COVID-19 pandemic when schools across Africa faced immense challenges transitioning to online learning, Lena addresses the specific needs of African educational systems, functioning reliably even in regions with limited internet connectivity.

Visit lena.africa to learn more about our mission to transform African education.
