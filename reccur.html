About This Project

Reccur creates virtual accounts for customers and helps them receive payments and disburse payments to their beneficiaries. Currently focused on Africa, they plan to expand and cover a larger base. The Reccur platform is built with speed, efficiency and flexibility for the user. It aims to be a one-stop shop for payments across international borders. Visit their website to learn more.
